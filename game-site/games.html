Games Section

Join us for an unforgettable day of gaming, fun, and entertainment.
We've got something for everyone, from casual gamers to hardcore. enthusiasts.

Ultimate Generation

In this section, which will be our biggest, the participants will have the opportunity to participate and play together all kinds of games of these last years, with different types of technology and different kinds of games for the public: Action-adventure, Shooters (FPS and TPS), Fighting games, Sports and Simulation, Role-playing (RPG, ARPG, and More), Multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA), Sandbox, Puzzlers and party games, and Rhythm games.

Classic Generation

This section is focused on the childhood games of almost part of our participants. We will have in this section the classic consoles, some are: Atari, Nintendo (NES), Master System, Mega Drive/Genesis, Super Nintendo, Sega Saturn, Nintendo 64 and PlayStation. They will play the classic main games of each console and revive their childhood experiences.

Arcade Generation

This is dedicated to the arcade games generation, which is focused on the older participants but all of you are invited to enjoy them. Will have the main classic games of the time, like Pac-Man, Space Invaders, Metal Slug, Asteroids, Donkey Kong, Street Fighter, and others.
