Content

Home page

Niflheim Game Event is an organized event with the objective to share the culture of the games with all people, inviting them to discover this world and participate in it. We appreciate all types of games in their own forms, we believe it is more than a hobby, it is discovering new worlds, exploring them, and living on them.

This site is about our next game event, that until now, will be the biggest event that we have organized, the objective of this home page is present the event, introduce some information about how it will be, and show some images of the things that we will have there. Here have all the information, without the details, that you need to know how it will be. The experience of this home page for our participants is to impact and seduce them to come and participate in the event, inviting also their family and friends.

Images for the Home page

Games

The objective of the games page is to show all the content of our event with details, to attract all kinds of people to participate. Separated in topics we will have three types of Attractions:

Ultimate Generation

In this event, which will be our biggest, the participants will have the opportunity to participate and play together all kinds of games of these last years, with different types of technology and different kinds of games for the public:
Action-adventure, Shooters (FPS and TPS), Fighting games, Sports and Simulation, Role-playing (RPG, ARPG, and More), Multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA), Sandbox, Puzzlers and party games, and Rhythm games.

Classic Games

This one is focused on the childhood games of almost part of our participants. We will have in this section the classic consoles: Nintendo (NES), Atari 7800, Master System, Mega Drive/Genesis, Super Nintendo, Sega Saturn, Nintendo 64, PlayStation, and PlayStation 2. They will play the classic main games of each console and revive their childhood experiences.

Arcade

This is dedicated to the arcade games generation, which is focused on the older participants but all of you are invited to enjoy them. Will have the main classic games of the time, like Pac-Man, Space Invaders, Metal Slug, Asteroids, Donkey Kong, Street Fighter, and others.
